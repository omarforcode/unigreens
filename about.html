About Us

Unigreens Company is a tight-knit group of friends who share a profound passion for global fruit and vegetable trade. Like a closely connected family, we collaborate to advance sustainable agriculture in Egypt and beyond. Our dual mission is to responsibly profit from the environment while nurturing a fresh produce culture. We aspire to provide the world with the chance to savor flawless fruits and vegetables, all while building a sustainable business. Furthermore, we actively engage with the local community, fostering growth and sustainable practices in agriculture.
